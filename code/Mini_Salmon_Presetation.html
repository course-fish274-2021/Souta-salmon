Finding

This presentation shows that bears benefit from the contribution of fisheries management in wildlife. Managing fisheries for wildlife does not limit salmon consumption. Data indicated an increased food supply for Bears from 1997 through 2018, supported by fisheries management data showing a trending uptick of increased Biomass (A Creek, C Creek, Hansen Creek, and Pedro Ponds). Furthermore, funding and continued supporting fisheries management studies of salmon species and tributary is essential in maintaining Mother nature’s food web.

Citation

Lincoln, A., Hilborn, R., Wirsing, A. J., & Quinn, T. P.. (2019). Managing salmon for wildlife: do fisheries limit salmon consumption by bears in small Alaskan streams? (Version 1). figshare. https://doi.org/10.6084/m9.figshare.10315925.v1 ([])
